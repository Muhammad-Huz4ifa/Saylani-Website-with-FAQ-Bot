Saylani Welfare is on the ground and already working with local communities to assess how best to support underprivileged families in more than 63 areas of day to day lives.

What We Are Doing

Saylani Welfare is providing multiple facilities to public.

Welfare

Saylani provides essential assistance and support to improve the well-being of individuals and communities.

Medical

Saylani provides medical assistance to underserved communities through its charitable initiatives.

Online Sadqah

Saylani facilitates online Sadqah contributions to support those in need worldwide.

Clean Water

Saylani provides access to clean water, ensuring communities have a fundamental resource for health and well-being.

Education

Saylani empowers communities through educational support, fostering brighter futures.

Food

Saylani provides essential food assistance to those facing hunger and food insecurity.

Hepatitis

Saylani Welfare has also set up a clinic for the best treatment of hepatitis patients where hepatitis patients are being treated
